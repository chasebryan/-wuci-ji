D0-D9

Conformance

Daylight conformance classifies evidence maturity. It does not certify security.

Levels

  1. D0 Research Artifact
  2. D1 Claim-Bounded
  3. D2 Evidence-Bound
  4. D3 Reproducible
  5. D4 Release-Gated
  6. D5 Control-Mapped
  7. D6 Continuously Monitored
  8. D7 Externally Reviewed
  9. D8 High-Assurance Product Candidate
  10. D9 Formal Authority Profile

Generate a Report

python3 tools/daylight_conformance.py status --project .

Read the report by checking blockers, forbidden claims, recommended next level, allowed claim, and forbidden claim.

D9 Rule

Wuci-Ji cannot self-issue external authority. D9 requires actual external certification, authorization, or regulatory authority evidence from the issuing body.
