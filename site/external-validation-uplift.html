External Validation Uplift

External review can strengthen authority only inside the scope it actually reviewed.

What It Changes

Reviewer trust, conformance level, audit authority, score confidence, adoption, and procurement readiness.

What It Does Not Change

No universal security, no permanent safety, no automatic government approval, and no certification outside the issuing scheme.

Accepted Evidence

Signed independent rebuilds, third-party review reports, red-team findings, falsification results, and actual certification records.

Attestation Requirements

Identity, scope, commit, artifacts, signature, scheme, public key, non-claim acknowledgement, and claim-usable flag.

D9 Authority Rule

D9 requires actual external certification, authorization, or regulatory authority. Wuci-Ji can carry the evidence but cannot self-issue D9.
