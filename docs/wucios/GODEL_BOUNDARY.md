# Gödel Boundary

Gödel Boundary defines what WuciOS does not claim, cannot prove locally, and must not imply.

WuciOS v2.4 is:

- not externally certified
- not production authorized
- not government approved
- not seL4 equivalent
- not formally verified unless future proof exists
- not a claim of perfect security
- not a claim that it cannot be hacked
- not a claim that reduced size alone proves security
- not a claim that any selected substrate is inherently high-assurance

The correct public language is: local evidence, candidate design, high-assurance-oriented, reviewable, not externally certified.

No document may imply approval by NSA, DoD, Department of War, seL4, NIST, NASA, any military body, any government body, any university, or any external auditor unless real evidence from that body exists and is in scope.
